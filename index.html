NEWPORT, RHODE ISLAND

RECOMMENDED ACCOMMODATIONS

Newport's beaches, its history and its food make it a popular place for folks to come and visit! Because May is the beginning of the summer season, rooms will fill up quickly so we recommend booking a place early.

Newport and the surrounding towns (Middletown and Portsmouth) offer a lot of options when it comes to accommodations. AirBnBs will offer the most variety when it comes to pricing, amenities, and locations. If you prefer a hotel or a traditional B&B experience, there are some great options too! You can find some recommended places below:

Hotel Viking

A boutique hotel in Newport where you can experience Newport's history. It's room rates are in the higher range, so better to book early before they climb for the summer season. (5 min drive from Newport Beach House).

https://www.hotelviking.com

Armistead Cottage

It's located in a quieter neighborhood right near some of the island's hot spots (Cliff Walk, First Beach, Newport Mansions). If you're interested in this option, please call to make your reservation (401) 889-4246, and let them know you're looking for a room for the Pathak-Dave wedding! (4 min drive from the wedding venue at Newport Beach House).

http://armisteadcottage.com


EAST ISLAND RESERVE HOTEL

Located at a distance of about a 12-minute drive to the wedding venue. Multiple room options and a great pool.

https://www.eastislandreserve.com

AirBnB Options

If you are planning to book early, AirBnB is a great option with a variety of places. You can find everything from one-bedroom apartments in downtown Newport to eight-bedroom houses on the beach for the entire family.

TRANSPORTATION DETAILS

As you plan your trip into the Newport area, you can find information about the closest airports and train stations along with car rental information here. Newport is on Aquidneck island off the southeast coast of the Rhode Island mainland. Once you’re on the island, distances to the weekend’s events and sightseeing locations are quite short, but getting to Newport from an airport or train station can take some coordinating. Car rentals are a great option to cover the distance, but ride sharing services are also dependable options. You can find information on both + approximate ride sharing costs below.

We know this may not answer all of your questions so please reach out to us any time as you finalize your plans! (amiandrutul@gmail.com or ashpath2000@yahoo.com)

Arrival By Plane: Closest Airports

  • Boston Logan (Boston, MA)
    Car rental recommended
    • Distance to Newport: 75 miles
    • Time to Newport: approx. 90 mins - 2 hours
  • TF Green Airport (Warwick, RI not located in Providence, RI as the airport code suggests!)
    Car rental or ride sharing an option
    • Distance to Newport: 27.5 miles
    • Time to Newport: ~ 33 mins
    • Ride sharing cost: ~ $38.00
  • JFK
    Car rental, train, or bus recommended
    • Distance to Newport: 183 miles
    • Time to Newport: ~ 3.5 hours

Arrival By Train: Closest Stations
